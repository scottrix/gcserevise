## CONTENT STRUCTURE FOR EACH TOPIC

For each topic within each subject/board, we need:

1. **Overview** - What the topic covers
2. **Key Concepts** - Core ideas and definitions
3. **Detailed Notes** - Comprehensive revision content
4. **Examples** - Real-world applications
5. **Common Exam Questions** - Typical question types
6. **Key Terms** - Glossary of essential vocabulary
7. **Practice Questions** - Self-assessment
8. **Exam Tips** - Board-specific advice

- Include diagrams, tables, and visual aids
- Mobile-first design for revision on phones
