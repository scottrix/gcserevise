Complete revision notes for GCSE Art and Design covering 38 topics across 6 titles. Aligned with AQA, Edexcel, OCR, Eduqas and CCEA specifications. Covers Fine Art, Graphic Communication, Textile Design, Three-Dimensional Design, Photography, and Art, Craft and Design — including assessment objectives, portfolio guidance and contextual studies.

📊 Course Overview

GCSE Art and Design is assessed through two components: a portfolio (Component 1, 60%) and an externally set assignment (Component 2, 40%). There is no written exam — both components are coursework-based. Students choose one or more titles from the six available. All titles share the same four Assessment Objectives (AO1–AO4), each worth 25%.

ComponentDescriptionWeighting
Component 1: PortfolioA sustained project developed from a personal starting point, plus supporting studies60%
Component 2: Externally Set AssignmentPreparatory period + 10 hours supervised time responding to an AQA starting point40%
