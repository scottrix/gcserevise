📊 Course Overview

GCSE Business (AQA 8132) is assessed through two written exams, each worth 50%. Both papers cover Business in the Real World and Influences on Business. Paper 1 also covers Business Operations and Human Resources; Paper 2 covers Marketing and Finance. Each paper has multiple choice, short answer and case study questions.

🧪 Key Topics

Enterprise: Entrepreneurship, business ownership, aims and objectives

Operations: Production processes, quality, customer service, procurement

Marketing: Market research, product life cycle, pricing, promotion

Finance: Sources of finance, cash flow, break-even, financial statements
