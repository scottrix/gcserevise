📊 GCSE Economics

Foundation Higher AQA 8136 & OCR J205

Complete revision notes for GCSE Economics covering 26 topics across 6 strands. Aligned with AQA 8136 and OCR J205 specifications. Covers Economic Foundations, Supply and Demand, Market Structures, the National Economy, Government Policy, and International Trade & Finance — including economic foundations, price determination, market failure, inflation, unemployment, fiscal and monetary policy, exchange rates, and globalisation.

📊 Course Overview

GCSE Economics explores how markets work and how the economy functions. AQA 8136 has 2 papers: Paper 1 (How Markets Work, 50%) and Paper 2 (How the Economy Works, 50%). OCR J205 has 2 components: Introduction to Economics (50%) and National & International Economics (50%). Both cover microeconomics (supply, demand, markets) and macroeconomics (government objectives, policy, trade).

📋 Board Coverage

All 26 topics are required for AQA 8136 and OCR J205. Each topic page indicates which board(s) require it. The core micro and macro content is common to both specifications.

🔬 Key Topics

Microeconomics: Supply and demand, price determination, elasticity, market structures, market failure

Macroeconomics: Economic growth, unemployment, inflation, balance of payments, government policy

Global Economy: International trade, exchange rates, globalisation, financial markets
