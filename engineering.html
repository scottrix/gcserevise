AQA 8852 & WJEC Eduqas 5799QA

Complete revision notes for GCSE Engineering covering 26 topics across 6 strands. Aligned with the AQA 8852 & WJEC Eduqas 5799QA specifications. Covers engineering materials, manufacturing processes, systems, testing, modern technologies and practical engineering skills.

⚙ Course Overview

GCSE Engineering develops students' understanding of engineering principles, materials and manufacturing processes. AQA 8852 has two components: Paper 1 (Written Exam, 60%, 2 hours) covering all subject content sections; and NEA (Non-Exam Assessment, 40%, approximately 30 hours) involving a practical engineering project with drawings, prototype and portfolio. WJEC Eduqas 5799QA has three units: Unit 1 (Manufacturing, internal), Unit 2 (Designing, internal) and Unit 3 (Solving Engineering Problems, 40%, 1hr 30min exam). Both test knowledge, application and evaluation.

📋 Board Coverage

All 26 topics are aligned with the AQA 8852 and WJEC Eduqas 5799QA specifications. AQA Paper 1 covers engineering materials, manufacturing processes, systems, testing, modern technologies and practical skills. WJEC Eduqas covers manufacturing, designing and solving engineering problems. Each topic page indicates the relevant specification content and provides calculation and application practice aligned with AO2 and evaluation practice aligned with AO3.

🔑 Key Topics

Engineering Materials: Metals, polymers, composites, material properties, selection and sustainability

Manufacturing Processes: Additive, subtractive, shaping, casting, joining and surface finishing processes

Systems: Mechanical, electrical, electronic, structural and pneumatic systems

Testing & Investigation: Modelling, calculation, testing methods, aerodynamics and quality control

Modern Technologies & Impact: Automation, Industry 4.0, social and environmental impact, ethics and sustainability

Practical Engineering Skills: Engineering drawings, CAD/CAM, production planning, problem-solving and assessment technique
