📊 Course Overview

GCSE English Language develops reading and writing skills across two papers. Students analyse fiction and non-fiction texts, and produce creative and transactional writing.

Strand Topics Focus
Reading Skills 8 Comprehension, analysis, evaluation
Creative Writing 5 Descriptive and narrative writing
Transactional Writing 6 Persuasive and non-fiction writing
SPaG 4 Spelling, punctuation and grammar
