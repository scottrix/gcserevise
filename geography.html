📊 Course Overview

GCSE Geography is assessed across 3 papers for AQA: Paper 1 (Physical Geography, 35%), Paper 2 (Human Geography, 35%) and Paper 3 (Geographical Applications, 30%). Foundation tier covers grades 1-5; Higher tier covers grades 4-9.
