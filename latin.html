📜 GCSE Latin

Foundation Higher OCR J282, Eduqas C990QS & AQA 8682

Complete revision notes for GCSE Latin covering 26 topics across 6 strands. Aligned with OCR J282, Eduqas C990QS, and AQA 8682 specifications. Covers Latin language (grammar, vocabulary, syntax), prose and verse literature, Roman civilisation, translation skills, and exam technique.

📜 Course Overview

GCSE Latin develops students' ability to read and understand Latin, appreciate its literature, and gain insight into Roman civilisation. OCR J282 has one compulsory Language component (50%) plus two optional components chosen from Prose Literature A/B, Verse Literature A/B, and Literature and Culture (each 25%). Eduqas C990QS has a Language component, Literature component, and Roman Civilisation component. AQA 8682 has a Language paper (50%), Literature (30%), and Roman Civilisation (20%). All three boards require translation from Latin to English, study of set texts in the original, and understanding of Roman culture.

📋 Board Coverage

All 26 topics are relevant across OCR J282, Eduqas C990QS, and AQA 8682. Each topic page indicates which board(s) require it. The core areas (language, literature, civilisation) are common to all three boards, though set texts differ between boards and change every two years. Translation skills and exam technique are universal requirements. Check your specification for current set text prescriptions.

🔑 Key Topics

Latin Language: Nouns & declensions, verbs & conjugations, cases & their uses, sentence structure, vocabulary & derivatives, subjunctive mood

Latin Literature (Prose): Set texts (Caesar, Cicero, Tacitus, Pliny), narrative & style, themes, exam technique

Latin Literature (Verse): Set texts (Virgil, Ovid, Catullus, Horace), poetic metre & style, themes, exam technique

Roman Civilisation: Daily life, entertainment, religion, government, the army & expansion

Translation Skills: Latin-to-English, English-to-Latin, comprehension & analysis, exam technique

Exam Technique: Language paper, literature paper, civilisation paper strategies
