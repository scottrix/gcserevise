🏃 GCSE Physical Education

Foundation Higher All Boards

Complete revision notes for GCSE PE covering 39 topics across 6 strands. Aligned with AQA, Edexcel, OCR, Eduqas and CCEA specifications. Covers Paper 1 (Anatomy and Physiology, Movement Analysis, Physical Training) and Paper 2 (Sports Psychology, Socio-Cultural Influences, Health and Wellbeing).

📊 Course Overview

GCSE PE is assessed through two written papers and a non-exam assessment (NEA). Paper 1 covers Applied Anatomy and Physiology, Movement Analysis and Physical Training. Paper 2 covers Sports Psychology, Socio-Cultural Influences and Health and Wellbeing. The NEA assesses practical performance in three activities.
