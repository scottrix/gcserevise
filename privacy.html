GCSE Revise is a free educational resource providing revision notes for GCSE students. This website is maintained by Scott Rix and is hosted on GitHub Pages.

2. What Data We Collect

We collect minimal data. Specifically:

  • Log data: GitHub Pages (our hosting provider) automatically collects standard server logs including IP address, browser type, referring page, and pages visited. We do not have direct access to these logs and do not use them to identify individuals.

We do not collect personal information such as names, email addresses, or phone numbers through this website, except where you voluntarily contact us by email.

This site is hosted on GitHub Pages, which provides HTTPS encryption for all connections. We do not store any personal data on our servers. No login or registration is required to use this site.

GCSE Revise is designed for students aged 14–16. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided personal information to us, please contact us and we will take steps to remove it.
