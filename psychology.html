🧠 GCSE Psychology

Foundation Higher AQA 8182, OCR J203 & Edexcel 1PS0

Complete revision notes for GCSE Psychology covering 26 topics across 6 strands. Aligned with AQA 8182, OCR J203, and Edexcel 1PS0 specifications. Covers Memory, Perception, Development, Social Influence, Language & Communication, Brain & Neuropsychology, Psychological Problems, Research Methods, and Key Approaches & Debates — including cognitive, biological, behavioural, and learning perspectives.

🧠 Course Overview

GCSE Psychology explores how the mind works and why people behave the way they do. AQA 8182 has 2 papers: Paper 1 "Cognition and Behaviour" (50%) covering Memory, Perception, Development and Research Methods; Paper 2 "Social Context and Behaviour" (50%) covering Social Influence, Language/Thought/Communication, Brain & Neuropsychology, and Psychological Problems. OCR J203 has 2 components: Studies and Applications in Psychology 1 (50%) and Studies and Applications in Psychology 2 (50%). Edexcel 1PS0 has 2 papers: Paper 1 (50%) and Paper 2 (50%).

📋 Board Coverage

All 26 topics are relevant across AQA 8182, OCR J203, and Edexcel 1PS0. Each topic page indicates which board(s) require it. While core content (memory, social influence, brain, psychological problems) is common to all three boards, there are differences in prescribed studies and optional topics. Check your specification for which studies you need to know in detail.

🔬 Key Topics

Cognitive Psychology: Memory models, perception, cognitive development

Social Psychology: Social influence, conformity, obedience, prosocial behaviour

Biological Psychology: Brain structure, neurons, nervous system, neuropsychology

Individual Differences: Mental health, depression, addiction, treatments

Research Methods: Experiments, observations, correlations, ethics, data analysis
