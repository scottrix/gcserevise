📊 Course Overview

GCSE Religious Studies (AQA 8062) is assessed through two written papers, each worth 50%. Paper 1 covers the study of religions — beliefs, teachings and practices (Christianity and Islam). Paper 2 covers thematic studies, applying religious and non-religious perspectives to contemporary ethical issues. Students must demonstrate knowledge (AO1) and the ability to analyse and evaluate (AO2).
