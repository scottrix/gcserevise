🇪🇸 GCSE Spanish

Foundation Higher All Boards

Complete revision notes for GCSE Spanish covering 32 topics across 3 themes plus grammar. Aligned with AQA, Edexcel, OCR, Eduqas and CCEA specifications. Covers Theme 1 (People and Lifestyle), Theme 2 (Popular Culture), Theme 3 (Communication and the World Around Us) and essential grammar.

📊 Course Overview

GCSE Spanish is assessed through four equally-weighted papers testing listening, speaking, reading and writing. All papers are set in the context of the three themes. Foundation tier covers grades 1-5; Higher tier covers grades 4-9.

PaperSkillsWeighting
