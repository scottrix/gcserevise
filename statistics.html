📊 GCSE Statistics

Edexcel 1ST0 & AQA 8382

Complete revision notes for GCSE Statistics covering 26 topics across 6 strands. Aligned with the Edexcel 1ST0 & AQA 8382 specifications. Covers planning enquiries, data collection, presentation, calculation, interpretation, probability and exam technique.

📊 Course Overview

GCSE Statistics develops students' ability to collect, analyse, interpret and evaluate statistical information. Edexcel 1ST0 and AQA 8382 both have two written papers, each 1 hour 45 minutes and worth 50%. Both papers assess the Statistical Enquiry Cycle (SEC) alongside statistical techniques, interpretation and evaluation. The content covers planning enquiries, data collection, presentation, calculation, interpretation and probability.

📋 Board Coverage

All 26 topics are aligned with the Edexcel 1ST0 and AQA 8382 specifications. Both boards follow the same DfE subject content covering planning, collection, presentation, calculation, interpretation and the Statistical Enquiry Cycle. Each topic page provides calculation practice aligned with AO1, interpretation practice aligned with AO2 and evaluation practice aligned with AO3.

🔑 Key Topics

Planning a Statistical Enquiry: Hypotheses, investigation design, constraints and the statistical enquiry cycle

Data Collection: Data types, primary and secondary data, sampling, questionnaires and data cleaning

Data Presentation & Visualisation: Tables, charts, graphs, histograms, box plots and identifying misrepresentation

Statistical Calculation & Analysis: Averages, spread, skewness, correlation, regression and time series analysis

Statistical Interpretation & Evaluation: Probability, distributions, index numbers, population estimation and comparing data

Exam Technique & SEC: SEC assessment, command words, statistical writing, calculations and revision
