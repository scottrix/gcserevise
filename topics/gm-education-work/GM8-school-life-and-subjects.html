❓ Practice Questions

Q1: Write 4 sentences in German about school life and subjects.

Q2: Write a paragraph (40-60 words) giving your opinion on school life and subjects.

Q3: Translate into German: "I think school life and subjects is important because..."

✅ Answers

  1. Four sentences using topic vocabulary with correct case endings, verb position and adjective endings. Include at least two different tenses for Higher tier.
  2. A structured paragraph: Meiner Meinung nach... (in my opinion), ich finde... weil... (I think... because...), aber/allerdings... (however), zusammenfassend... (in summary). Use connecting words and varied vocabulary.
  3. "Ich finde, dass school life and subjects wichtig ist, weil..." — ensure correct word order (verb at end after dass), correct case endings, and give a specific reason.

🎯 Exam Tips

  • Use a range of tenses (present, past, future) to access higher marks
  • Include opinions with reasons (ich finde... weil... / meiner Meinung nach...)
  • Check case endings (nominative, accusative, dative) before finishing
  • Get word order right: verb-second in main clauses, verb-at-end after dass/weil/wenn
  • Use complex structures: subordinate clauses, modal verbs, separable verbs
  • Include cultural references to German-speaking countries (DACH)

⚠️ Common Errors

Watch Out!

Students often forget word order after subordinating conjunctions. Wrong: Ich denke, dass ich will Arzt werden. Correct: Ich denke, dass ich Arzt werden will (verb goes to the END after dass, weil, wenn, obwohl).

Detailed Notes

School Subjects and Opinions

At GCSE level, you need to name school subjects and give opinions about them. Key subjects: Mathe(matik) (maths), Englisch (English), Deutsch (German), Geschichte (history), Geografie (geography), die Naturwissenschaften (sciences), Biologie, Chemie, Physik, Kunst (art), Musik (music), Sport (PE), Informatik (computer science). Note that school subjects in German are always capitalised as they are nouns.

To express opinions about subjects, use a range of structures: Ich mag Mathe, weil es interessant ist (I like maths because it is interesting). Ich hasse Geschichte, weil es langweilig ist (I hate history because it is boring). Mein Lieblingsfach ist Physik (My favourite subject is physics). Ich bin gut in Englisch (I am good at English). Ich bin schlecht in Chemie (I am bad at chemistry). The preposition in takes the dative for school subjects: in + dem = im.

The German school system is different from the UK. After primary school (die Grundschule, years 1–4), pupils are divided into different school types: das Gymnasium (academic school, leads to das Abitur), die Realschule (intermediate school) and die Hauptschule (basic school). In Austria, the system is similar with das Gymnasium and die Mittelschule. In Switzerland, cantons decide their own systems.

Beispiel

Mein Lieblingsfach ist Mathe, weil ich Zahlen mag und der Lehrer sehr gut erklärt. Ich hasse Kunst, weil ich nicht gut zeichnen kann. Am besten finde ich aber Informatik.
My favourite subject is maths because I like numbers and the teacher explains very well. I hate art because I cannot draw well. But I think computer science is the best.

The School Day in Germany

The German school day is structured differently from the UK. School typically starts at 7:30 or 8:00 AM and lessons last 45 minutes. There is a long break (die große Pause) of 15–20 minutes mid-morning. Traditionally, German schools finished at lunchtime (die Halbtagsschule), but die Ganztagsschule (full-day school) is becoming more common, with afternoon activities and homework supervision (die Hausaufgabenbetreuung).

Key school vocabulary includes: der Unterricht (lesson/teaching), die Stunde (lesson/period), die Hausaufgaben (homework), das Zeugnis (school report), die Note (grade/mark — in Germany, 1 is the best and 6 is the worst), die Klassenarbeit (class test), die Prüfung (exam), das Schuljahr (school year), das Pausenbrot (break-time snack).

In Austria, school also often ends at lunchtime, with die Nachmittagsbetreuung (afternoon care) available for working parents. Swiss schools typically have a longer lunch break (1–2 hours) where children go home. Understanding these differences from the British system is valuable for the intercultural element of the exam.

Beispiel

In meiner Schule beginnt der Unterricht um acht Uhr. Wir haben sechs Stunden pro Tag. In der großen Pause esse ich mein Pausenbrot. Die Hausaufgaben mache ich zu Hause.
In my school lessons begin at eight o'clock. We have six periods per day. During the long break I eat my break-time snack. I do my homework at home.

Describing Your School

To describe your school, use adjectives and the correct case endings. Key phrases: Meine Schule ist groß/klein (My school is big/small), Sie liegt in der Stadtmitte/auf dem Land (It is located in the city centre/in the countryside), Sie hat einen Sportplatz/eine Turnhalle (It has a sports field/a gym), Es gibt eine Bibliothek und eine Mensa (There is a library and a canteen).

Use es gibt (there is/are) + accusative to describe facilities: Es gibt einen Computerraum (There is a computer room). Use man kann (one can) to describe activities: Man kann im Sportverein Fußball spielen (One can play football in the sports club). For Higher tier, use relative clauses: Meine Schule, die im Zentrum liegt, hat über 800 Schüler (My school, which is in the centre, has over 800 pupils).

German schools often have a strong Partnerschaft (partnership) with schools in other countries, especially for language exchanges (der Schüleraustausch). Many German students do an exchange with a British school. This is an excellent topic for the speaking exam as it allows you to compare both school systems.

Beispiel

Meine Schule ist ziemlich groß und liegt in der Stadtmitte. Es gibt eine Turnhalle, einen Sportplatz und eine Bibliothek. Man kann viele AGs besuchen, zum Beispiel Theater-AG und Sport-AG.
My school is quite big and is located in the city centre. There is a gym, a sports field and a library. You can attend many clubs, for example drama club and sports club.

Additional Practice Questions

Q1: Beschreibe deine Schule und dein Schulleben. Welche Fächer magst du und warum? (Describe your school and school life. Which subjects do you like and why?)

Q2: Übersetze ins Deutsche: My favourite subject is German because the teacher is very good. I am bad at maths. There is a library and a sports hall at my school.

Model Answers

  1. Ich besuche ein großes Gymnasium in München. Der Unterricht beginnt um acht Uhr und ich habe meistens sechs Stunden pro Tag. Mein Lieblingsfach ist Physik, weil es interessant ist und ich Naturwissenschaften mag. Ich hasse jedoch Geschichte, weil es mir langweilig vorkommt. In der großen Pause treffe ich meine Freunde und wir essen zusammen in der Mensa. Nach der Schule mache ich meine Hausaufgaben und dann gehe ich zum Sporttraining.

    (I attend a large Gymnasium in Munich. Lessons begin at eight o'clock and I usually have six periods per day. My favourite subject is physics because it is interesting and I like sciences. However, I hate history because it seems boring to me. During the long break I meet my friends and we eat together in the canteen. After school I do my homework and then I go to sports training.)
  2. Mein Lieblingsfach ist Deutsch, weil der Lehrer sehr gut ist. Ich bin schlecht in Mathe. Es gibt eine Bibliothek und eine Turnhalle in meiner Schule.
