R4: Simplifying Ratio

Foundation Higher All Boards

Use ratio notation; reduce ratios to simplest form

📋 Key Concepts

Definition: A ratio compares two or more quantities. The ratio a:b means 'a parts to b parts'.

Ratio Notation

  • Written as a:b (for two quantities)
  • Written as a:b:c (for three quantities)
  • Order matters - 3:5 is different from 5:3
To simplify: Divide all parts by their highest common factor (HCF).

📝 Simplifying Ratios

Method: Find the HCF of all numbers in the ratio, then divide each by the HCF.
Example 1

Simplify the ratio 12:18

Solution:

HCF of 12 and 18 is 6

12 ÷ 6 = 2, 18 ÷ 6 = 3

Answer: 2:3

Example 2

Simplify the ratio 24:36:12

Solution:

HCF of 24, 36 and 12 is 12

24 ÷ 12 = 2, 36 ÷ 12 = 3, 12 ÷ 12 = 1

Answer: 2:3:1

📝 Ratios with Decimals and Fractions

Method: Convert to whole numbers first by multiplying, then simplify.
Example 3

Simplify the ratio 2.5:3.5

Solution:

Multiply by 10: 25:35

HCF of 25 and 35 is 5

25 ÷ 5 = 5, 35 ÷ 5 = 7

Answer: 5:7

Example 4

Simplify the ratio 3/4 : 1/2

Solution:

Convert to common denominator: 3/4 : 2/4

Multiply by 4: 3:2

Answer: 3:2

📝 Ratios with Units

Method: Convert to the same units first, then simplify.
Example 5

Simplify the ratio 50 cm : 2 m

Solution:

Convert: 2 m = 200 cm

Ratio: 50:200

HCF = 50

50 ÷ 50 = 1, 200 ÷ 50 = 4

Answer: 1:4

Example 6

Simplify the ratio 30 minutes : 2 hours

Solution:

Convert: 2 hours = 120 minutes

Ratio: 30:120

HCF = 30

Answer: 1:4

📝 Equivalent Ratios

Equivalent Ratios: Ratios that simplify to the same ratio. Multiply or divide all parts by the same number.
Example 7

Find two equivalent ratios to 4:7

Solution:

Multiply by 2: 8:14

Multiply by 3: 12:21

Answer: 8:14 and 12:21

❓ Practice Questions

Q1: Simplify the ratio 16:24

Q2: Simplify the ratio 45:30:15

Q3: Simplify the ratio 1.5:2.5:4

Q4: Simplify the ratio 75 cm : 1.5 m

Q5: Write three ratios equivalent to 2:5

✅ Answers

  1. 2:3
  2. 3:2:1
  3. 3:5:8 (multiply by 10 to get 15:25:40, then divide by 5)
  4. 1:2 (150 cm : 150 cm after conversion, or 75:150 = 1:2)
  5. 4:10, 6:15, 8:20 (any three multiples)

🎯 Exam Tips

  • Always convert to whole numbers first if there are decimals or fractions
  • Convert to the same units before simplifying
  • Find the HCF to simplify in one step
  • The numbers in a simplified ratio should have no common factors
  • Check your answer by multiplying back to see if you get the original
